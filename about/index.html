About

HU Cybersecurity Web Academy

Community-driven training created by the HU Cybersecurity Community at the University of Hebron.

Mission

Equip HU students with practical web security skills, emphasizing ethical testing, accessible tooling, and collaborative learning.

How we operate

  • Static-first approach — no logins, minimal hosting needs.
  • External labs via PortSwigger, TryHackMe, HackTheBox.
  • Mentor review at the end of each track level.
  • Community office hours Wednesdays 14:00–16:00 (UTC+2).
