Rubrics & Grading Criteria

Use these transparent rubrics to self-assess before submitting labs or the capstone.

Single Lab Rubric (100 pts · pass 70)

Criterion Description Points
Goal understanding Explains what the lab is proving and scope boundaries. 10
PoC execution & evidence Shows working payloads with screenshots or captured requests. 40
Evidence quality Clear, annotated, reproducible artefacts. 20
Clarity of write-up Logical structure, concise explanation, ties to impact. 15
Ethics & safety Confirms testing bounds, no harmful actions, responsible behavior. 10
Timeliness Delivered within agreed deadline with version control. 5

Capstone Rubric (100 pts · pass 80)

Criterion Description Points
Recon & Scope Clear objectives, inventory, and documented assumptions. 15
Vulnerability discovery Multiple validated findings mapped to recognized risks. 20
PoC / Exploit execution Detailed exploitation steps with supporting evidence. 25
Report structure & clarity Professional layout, executive summary, prioritized remediation. 20
Ethics & Disclosure Adherence to ROE, disclosure plan, respectful communication. 10
Supporting materials Appendices, logs, and artefacts attached. 10

TA Checklist

  • Confirm student used the latest template version.
  • Verify scoring math and provide narrative feedback.
  • Return annotated PDF via email within 5 business days.
