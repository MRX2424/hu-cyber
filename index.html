How it works

Move through each level with a clear rhythm. Lessons build understanding, labs reinforce skills, quizzes confirm readiness, and the capstone demonstrates professional competency.

1. Learn

Study concise lessons with objectives, demos, and mini-checks.

2. Practice

Apply concepts in safe, external labs with guided tasks and hints.

3. Quiz

Check mastery with self-graded quizzes before advancing levels.

4. Capstone

Deliver a real-world style report, reviewed by TAs and mentors.
