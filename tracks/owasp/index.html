Track

OWASP / Web Exploits

Investigate the OWASP Top 10 and beyond through ethical exploitation labs, progressive levels, and strong remediation writing practice.

Levels & outcomes

Level 1

Essentials

Identify and reproduce reflected XSS, stored XSS, SQLi, and traversal flaws in safe labs.

  • Lesson: OWASP Top 10 orientation
  • Lab: Reflected XSS on training app
  • Lesson: Simple SQLi pattern spotting
  • Quiz: L1 vulnerabilities

Level 2

Chaining

Move from single issues to chaining SSRF, auth bypass, and DOM-based attacks with ethical constraints.

  • Lesson: DOM XSS detection
  • Lab: Blind SQLi (time-based)
  • Lesson: IDOR triage
  • Quiz: Scenario-based responses

Level 3

Capstone prep

Run a full pentest in a dedicated range, practice WAF evasion, and deliver refined remediation write-ups.

  • Lab: Multi-stage exploit chain
  • Lesson: Deserialization pitfalls
  • Workshop: Prioritizing bounty submissions
