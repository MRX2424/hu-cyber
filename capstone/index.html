Capstone Project

Capstone Brief & Templates

Deliver a professional web penetration test report on a provided vulnerable application. Demonstrate methodology, ethical considerations, and clear remediation guidance.

Scenario & Scope

Target

  • URL: https://academy-capstone.hebron.edu
  • Environment: Dedicated training VM (no real data)
  • Credentials: Provided by mentor (read-only)

Rules of engagement

  • Testing window: 72 hours
  • No destructive payloads (DoS, brute force)
  • Follow Ethics policy

Deliverables

  • Completed capstone report (PDF) using provided template.
  • Evidence folder (screenshots, requests/responses).
  • Timeline summary with hours spent and key milestones.

Rubric (100 pts · pass 80)

Recon & Scope · 15 pts

Clear target description, asset inventory, and documented limitations.

Vulnerability discovery · 20 pts

Multiple validated findings mapped to OWASP Top 10 or equivalent risks.

PoC & Exploitation · 25 pts

Detailed steps, payloads, and evidence supporting each finding.

Report structure · 20 pts

Professional formatting, executive summary, and prioritized remediation.

Ethics & Disclosure · 10 pts

Adherence to responsible disclosure guidelines and communication plan.

Supporting materials · 10 pts

Complete attachments and reproducible artefacts.
