Ethics

Responsible Disclosure & Conduct

Pentesting skills require trust. Follow these principles to protect real users and maintain access to training platforms.

Do

  • Test only approved labs and ranges.
  • Respect authentication boundaries.
  • Document steps and impact carefully.
  • Report findings promptly to mentors.

Don't

  • Attack production systems or classmates.
  • Exfiltrate, modify, or destroy data.
  • Share lab credentials publicly.
  • Bypass mentor instructions or ROE.
